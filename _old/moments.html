The Core Technology

What is Moments?

Moments is FOG's proprietary AI system that lives inside every venue. Overhead cameras and intelligent scoring algorithms work silently in the background — capturing every clutch play, celebrating every peak performance, and delivering a shareable highlight reel directly to each player's phone before they even leave the floor.

No operator effort required. No player setup needed. Pure, automatic magic.

01

Real-Time Capture

Multi-angle overhead cameras record gameplay at 60fps. Every frame is tagged with player identity, timestamp, and in-game context.

02

AI Scoring Engine

Our model evaluates every play against a skill matrix — intensity, reaction time, coordination, and crowd energy — generating a highlight score in milliseconds.

03

Instant Delivery

Players scan a QR code at the exit and receive a branded, social-ready video clip sent directly to their device — no app download required.
