## Why LED Gaming Floors Are Dominating FECs

The family entertainment centre industry is evolving rapidly. Players are no longer satisfied with passive entertainment — they want to *be* in the game. This shift has created a clear winner in the attraction equipment space: sensor-activated LED gaming floors.

HyperGrid, FOG Technologies' flagship LED floor system, has been deployed in over 40 venues across India, Australia, Singapore, and the Gulf. The numbers from these installations tell a consistent story.

## The Numbers Behind the Investment

Operators who install HyperGrid report three consistent outcomes within the first 90 days:

- **Dwell time increases by 35–50%** — players stay longer because the floor is dynamic, not static
- **Repeat visit rate climbs to 65–70%** — the rotating game modes mean there is always something new to play
- **Revenue per square foot doubles** — a 400 sq ft HyperGrid installation outperforms a 1,200 sq ft traditional soft play area in revenue

The median ROI payback period across our installed base is **8.2 months**. The fastest we have seen was 5 months, in a high-footfall mall venue in Bangalore.

## The Four Game Modes That Drive Engagement

HyperGrid ships with four core game modes, and operators can activate them independently or rotate them on a schedule.

**1. Step-It** — A memory and reaction game where the floor lights up sequences for players to follow. Works for all age groups, competitive, and requires no instruction.

**2. Cluster Crush** — Team-based competition where groups of players must occupy lit zones simultaneously. Drives group bookings and birthday party packages.

**3. Speed Grid** — Individual timed challenge. Perfect for competitive players who want a leaderboard metric. Pairs well with Moments AI for highlight clip delivery.

**4. Free Zone** — An open creative mode where the floor responds to footsteps with colour patterns. Popular with younger children and as a cool-down mode between competitive rounds.
